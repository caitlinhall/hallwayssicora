Speed Dating

During Welcome Week at UST, Anthony and Caitlin met at a school sponsored, freshman speed dating event for each other's dorm floors. Jack Pauly, Anthony’s childhood friend, walks into the room and introduces Anthony as the Italian Stallion. After a lovely 5 minute conversation between Caitlin and Anthony, the bell rang, and from that time on Caitlin called Anthony the Italian Stallion.

First Date

After some completely awkward chit-chat about whether they should go left, down the "friendship" path, or right, down toward "something more", they decided to go right. In other words, they decided to go on their first date. Anthony took Caitlin to the Chatter Box where they played a fun game of Connect Four. Caitlin knocked down all her chips, which flew everywhere. To make Caitlin feel better, Anthony did the same. What a good guy.

4/22/2010

First Kiss

Caitlin asked Anthony to go to formal with her. They danced the night away. As the night came to an end, Caitlin pointed to her cheek asking for a kiss, and Anthony obliged. Then Caitlin pointed to her other cheek, and Anthony again obliged. Finally, Caitlin pointed to her lips and that was their first kiss.

5/8/2010

True Tommies

One chilly, Spring night, Anthony told Caitlin he had a surprise and wanted to go for a walk at 11:55pm. Caitlin secretly knew something was about to happen. They walked through campus and they went to the arches, a St. Thomas landmark where, tradition states, new couples will become soul mates by kissing one another underneath the arches at midnight. They kiss and then became True Tommies by following the arch’s tradition.

10/19/2013

Popped the Question

Anthony and Caitlin went to Tulum, Mexico, also known as the Walled City. Tulum stands on a bluff facing towards the Caribbean Sea. The ruins at Tulum have three walls creating a fort. They walked through the ruins at Tulum, came to the end of one of its walls and continued out to get a better view of the sea. Unbeknownst to Caitlin, Anthony and his bother, Tim, had planned for a special question to be asked to her at this exact spot. The planned cue was for Anthony to ask Tim to take a picture of them. Tim, the most awesome little brother, suggested to Anthony, “Hey Anthony, you want a picture here.” Anthony agreed that this was a good spot for a photo. Tim snapped a picture of Caitlin and Anthony by the sea and Caitlin continued walking eagerly to the next part of the ruins, not knowing what was about to happen. Anthony grabbed her hand and said “wait a second, I got a question for you.” Caitlin, in confusion and lack of patience, said, “no you don’t”, and continued to walk away. Anthony proclaimed he was serious and got down on one knee. Caitlin, in utter shock, stood there and squealed “YES!”, but would not remove her hands from her mouth. Anthony asked, “well can I have that hand to put the ring on?” Caitlin then said, “yes, of course”, with a giant smile.
